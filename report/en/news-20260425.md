## Future

Three predictions derived from this report.

1. **The "frontier AI lab × Tier-1 cloud capacity capture" splits the field cleanly by Q3 2026.** With Google Cloud confirming three deals in a single month — Anthropic (GB300 + large TPU), Meta (GB300 capacity), Thinking Machines Lab (A4X Max) — the structure crystallizes: clouds with enough capacity for frontier model development narrow to three (AWS / Azure / GCP). Amazon counters with **another $25B + 5GW Trainium2/3 capacity + 10-year $100B commit** for Anthropic; Azure holds OpenAI / Mistral / Inflection; GCP adds Anthropic / TML / Meta. By Q3 2026, "fourth-hyperscaler candidates without their own cloud or long-term capacity contracts with frontier labs" (Oracle Cloud / IBM / Coreweave) shift fully to **"unable to train frontier-scale, specialize on inference."** AI silicon ecosystems lock into a two-tier shape: training capacity = three-cloud oligopoly / inference = layered.
2. **"Physical AI production-line records become league tables at IROS / GTC 2026" starts H2 2026.** With Siemens Erlangen's **HMND 01 Alpha** (60 tote/h × 8h × 90%+), BMW Spartanburg's **Figure 02** (10 months / 30,000 vehicles / 90,000 parts), BMW Leipzig's **AEON** (Apr 2026 trial → summer pilot), and GXO's **Digit** (100K+ totes) all hit the same window — and Hannover Messe 2026 making Physical AI its first central theme — the industry's evaluation criterion is shifting from "vendor-claimed demos" to **"OEM-adopted production records."** In H2 2026 at **IROS (October)** and **GTC Fall 2026**, league tables for "OEM adoption count × continuous-operation hours × parts handled × MTBF" begin, with Apptronik Apollo / Agility Digit / Figure 03 / Humanoid HMND 01 / Hexagon AEON's top five at the top. The 30+ companies still stuck in PoC phase get pushed into Q4 2026 funding shortfalls / acquisitions / exits.
3. **"Attack surface against the MCP protocol itself" becomes the biggest enterprise AI adoption bottleneck by Q3 2026.** Nginx UI's **CVE-2026-33032 (MCPwn, CVSS 9.8)** — a structural defect where the `/mcp_message` endpoint, with its empty default IP allowlist, exposes 12 unauthenticated tool calls — was added to VulnCheck KEV after a 2-request real-world takeover in production. That tells us the MCP protocol itself needs to be redesigned as a web standard if it's going to scale. By Q3 2026, OWASP MCP Top 10 will be officially published, **MCP-over-mTLS / standardized OAuth scope specs by Anthropic / Microsoft / Google / Cisco / Cloudflare** will become industry standard, and Keycard / Okta for AI Agents / Wiz / Levo.ai / Pillar Security will roll out "MCP Gateway / MCP Firewall" products through Q4. Enterprise compliance audits will start tracking "number of MCP-connected tools / endpoints / authorization policies" as a new audit category.
